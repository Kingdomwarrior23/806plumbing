Lubbock Plumbing Emergencies Don't Wait. Neither Do We.

Lubbock's climate is brutal on plumbing. Winter northers drop temperatures from 65°F to 10°F in hours — and homes built for West Texas summers aren't designed for it. Pipes in garages, exterior walls, and crawl spaces freeze fast. One hard freeze can burst three pipes in the same house.

Summer is different but just as punishing. Extreme heat causes ground shift, which stresses underground lines. High mineral content in Lubbock water corrodes water heaters years before their rated lifespan.

806 Plumbing was built here because we know these problems. Every plumber in our network has worked Lubbock soil, dealt with caliche-clogged lines, and fixed freeze damage in the same neighborhoods. This isn't a national directory — it's your neighbor with a wrench.

Common Lubbock Plumbing Questions

Real answers to what Lubbock homeowners ask most.

Immediately shut off your main water valve — in most Lubbock homes it's near the meter at the street or inside near the water heater. Then call a licensed plumber. In freezing conditions, pipes in exterior walls and garages are most vulnerable. A plumber can repipe the burst section and check for freeze damage elsewhere in 2-4 hours. Don't wait — even a small burst can cause $10,000+ in water damage within an hour.

Lubbock gets hard freezes several times a year — the flat terrain gives no wind protection and temperatures can drop from 70°F to 10°F in hours during a norther. Homes built for West Texas summers often have poor pipe insulation in garages, exterior walls, and crawl spaces. A single freeze event can burst multiple pipes simultaneously, especially in homes built before 1980.

Most standard Texas homeowner's policies cover sudden and accidental water damage from a burst pipe — but not the pipe repair itself. The key is acting fast and documenting everything. Call your insurer immediately, take photos before any cleanup, and get a written repair estimate from the plumber. Some insurers require a licensed plumber's report to process the claim.
